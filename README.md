# Interactive-Sales-Analytics-Dashboard-in-Excel

## Project Overview
This project is an interactive Excel Sales Dashboard designed to analyze annual sales performance, customer behavior, regional contribution, and sales channel effectiveness. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights using dynamic visualizations, KPI cards, and interactive slicers.

---

# Business Problem
The company faced difficulties in identifying its actual customer segmentation, high-performing sales channels, top-performing states, and customer age groups. Due to the lack of clear analytical insights, management struggled to create effective marketing strategies, promotional campaigns, and distribution planning.

---

# Project Objective
The main objective of this project is to:
- Analyze annual sales performance
- Identify target customer segments
- Discover high-performing sales channels
- Evaluate state-wise sales contribution
- Understand customer age-group behavior
- Support data-driven business decisions

---

# Dashboard Features
- Revenue KPI Card
- Orders KPI Card
- Gross Profit KPI Card
- Profit Margin KPI Card
- Monthly Orders vs Sales Trend
- Gender-wise Sales Analysis
- Order Status Distribution
- Top 5 States by Sales
- Orders vs Age Group Analysis
- Sales Channel Performance
- Interactive Slicers & Filters

---

# Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Slicers
- Conditional Formatting
- Data Cleaning
- Dashboard Design
- Data Visualization

---

# Key Insights
- Women customers contribute approximately 65% of total sales.
- Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the top 3 performing states contributing nearly 35% of total sales.
- The Adult age group (30–49 years) is the highest contributing customer segment with around 50% contribution.
- Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the leading sales channels contributing nearly 80% of total revenue and orders.
- Most orders were successfully delivered, indicating strong operational performance.

---

# Business Impact
This dashboard helped the business:
- Identify profitable customer segments
- Improve marketing and promotional strategy
- Focus on high-performing sales channels
- Optimize regional distribution planning
- Improve decision-making through data-driven insights
